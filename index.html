Navchetna Mahila Manch

Empowering women through education, environmental awareness, and better health for families and communities.

Navchetna Mahila Manch (NMM) is a women‑led NGO dedicated to spreading awareness about education, environment, and health among children, youth, and women. We create safe learning spaces, organize campaigns, and connect communities with resources that help them live informed, dignified, and sustainable lives.

About Us

Navchetna Mahila Manch is a platform of literary, hardworking, enthusiastic, cooperative, and respected women who continuously strive to bring positive change in society. Through their persistent efforts, they have achieved remarkable milestones that serve as an inspiration to others.

The guiding force behind Navchetna Mahila Manch was Mrs. Madhur Chandra, a woman of great vision and determination who transformed her dreams into reality. Along with Mrs. Madhuri Kulshreshtha, Mrs. Nirmala Bhatnagar, Mrs. Savitri Verma, and Mrs. Manju Kansal, she laid the foundation of this strong organization.

After more than a year of dedicated efforts, these hardworking women, together with other members, formally established the group on 6 October 1998 under the name "Navchetna Mahila Manch" and got it registered. The organization consists exclusively of women, including homemakers and professionals such as doctors, police officers, engineers, principals, yoga instructors, teachers, singers, editors, and talented women skilled in arts, science, dance, and religious activities.

One day, while travelling, the members observed some poor children engaging in undesirable activities. This deeply disturbed them. After much discussion and reflection, they concluded that only education could improve the lives of such children. Therefore, the organization took up the responsibility of educating children who were often neglected even by their own parents.

With the suggestion of Mrs. Prem Nair and the approval of all members, a plan was made to open an educational centre. Children of domestic workers, labourers, and rickshaw pullers were invited to join, and on 5 September 1999, the "Navchetna Literacy Centre" was established.

This marked the beginning of the organization's mission to spread education and create a brighter future for underprivileged children.

Our Key Initiatives

Education for Every Child

Navchetna Mahila Manch runs reading and learning initiatives that encourage children to build strong reading habits and expand their minds. Through book‑based activities, storytelling, and study support, we help children see education as an adventure that shapes their dreams and futures.

Learn more

Environment & Climate Awareness

On occasions like World Environment Day, we organize drives, campaigns, and school activities to build awareness about environmental protection. Practical steps include tree plantation, waste segregation, and conserving local resources.

Learn more

Health & Well‑being

Our health‑focused sessions and community meetings spread awareness about hygiene, nutrition, and preventive care, especially for women and children. We collaborate with local experts and partners to make critical health information simple and actionable.

Learn more

Community Engagement & Culture

We regularly host community gatherings, cultural programs, and group activities that bring women, youth, and children together. These spaces help build confidence, leadership skills, and a strong sense of belonging among participants.
